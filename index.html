TALENTA Campus Talent & Content Information SystemI

Item-Rift

Item-Rift is a web-based marketplace platform specifically designed to facilitate peer-to-peer transactions of pre-owned goods among university students. The platform addresses the lack of a centralized, trusted system for students to buy and sell second-hand items such as books, electronics, academic supplies, and furniture—items that are often still functional but underutilized.

The application was developed to promote safe, sustainable, and affordable commerce in a campus environment, with a focus on circular economy practices. It reduces waste and encourages the reuse of goods while helping students save money and form a supportive community around responsible consumption.

Features:

  • User Authentication & Role Management
  • Item Listing & Management
  • Messaging System
  • Forum Module
  • User Profile Management
  • Admin Dashboard
